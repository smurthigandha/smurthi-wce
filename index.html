About Us

Welcome to the official website of the Insaaf Foundation's new initiative, "Smurthi". Smurthi is an educational program aimed at providing free education to underprivileged individuals who cannot afford it. Our foundation believes that education is a fundamental right of every human being, regardless of their financial situation.
