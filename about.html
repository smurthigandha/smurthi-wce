About Us

Smruthi is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to working with underprivileged children, students, orphans, and those in need in the fields of education and social service. We believe that every individual has the right to quality education and basic necessities of life, and we strive to make a difference in their lives.

Welcome to Smruthi Since - 1898

Our mission is to provide educational and social support to the less fortunate members of our community, especially children and students who face challenges in accessing education and basic necessities due to poverty and other circumstances. We believe that education is a fundamental right, and we work to ensure that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background.

At Smruthi, we believe that our children are the future, and investing in their education and well-being is crucial to building a better world. We provide a range of services, including educational support, healthcare, nutrition, and psychological support to ensure that every child can grow and thrive in a safe and nurturing environment. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to support our cause, and we partner with local communities and organizations to achieve our goals. We believe that working together can create lasting change and make a positive impact in the lives of those we serve.

If you are passionate about education and social service, we invite you to join us in our mission. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of those in need and help build a brighter future for all.
